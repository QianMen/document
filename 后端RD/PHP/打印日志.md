######1.打印变量

file_put_contents('/tmp/yanzhou.log',
    "\n\n".'Time:'.date('Y-m-d H:i:s',time())."------START--------------------------------------------------------------------------------"
    ."\n".__NAMESPACE__.'\\'.__CLASS__.'|'.__FUNCTION__.':'.__LINE__."\n".

######2.打印对象所属的类拥有的方法名

```php
file_put_contents('/tmp/yanzhou.log',
    "\n\n".'Time:'.date('Y-m-d H:i:s',time())."------START--------------------------------------------------------------------------------"
    ."\n".__NAMESPACE__.'\\'.__CLASS__.'|'.__FUNCTION__.':'.__LINE__."\n".
    var_export(get_class_methods( get_class( $object ) ),1)
    ."\n-------------------------------END---------------------------------------------------------------------------------\n", 8);
```
